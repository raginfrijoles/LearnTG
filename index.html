TDT1 E-Portfolio

Rates, Proportions, and Ratios

Welcome

This is an e-portfolio created by Thomas Gallegos for the course "TDT1: Technology Portfolio" at Western Governors University. The theme of this portfolio will center around fractional relationships like: rates, proportions, and ratios.

Goal of Instruction

Given a word problem or in-class scenario, 7th grade students at St. Charles Catholic School will be able to identify the relationship between given information and a variable, as well as convert rational numbers to like terms in order to calculate unit rates, ratios, and percentages by scoring 75% or higher on in-class assessments.
